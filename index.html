About the Event

Celebrating culture, photography and creativity.

Surarada Saman Pelahara

Surarada Saman Pelahara is an annual inter-school photography marathon conducted during the sacred Saman Devalaya Perahera in Ratnapura, Sri Lanka.

Student photographers from across the island capture the vibrant traditions, lights, dancers, elephants and cultural heritage of the procession, before editing and submitting their work for professional judging.
